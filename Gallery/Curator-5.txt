Lisa Sharkey|45|SVP, HarperCollins Publishers|Lisa Sharkey is the Senior Vice President and Director of Creative Development for HarperCollins Publishers where she acquires high-profile books from important figures in current events and popular culture. Prior to joining HarperCollins, Lisa worked in television in such roles as the Senior Producer for ABC’s Good Morning America. During her television career, Lisa won two Emmy Awards, is a ten-time Emmy nominee, and has been honored by a Peabody Award and a DuPont Award for her work at ABC covering the events surrounding September 11th.

On her curated Offset collection, Lisa said, “these images represent what brings me joy in my life - books, babies, spiritual pursuits, flowers, architecture and four-legged creatures.”
