Harley Viera-Newton|35|Deejay, model, MMA Fighter|Harley Viera-Newton is a deejay and model. Born in London and raised in Los Angeles, she was signed by Jay Z's RocNation and now, in addition to regular sets at hot spots like Westway, The Jane, 1Oak and Le Bain, she deejays all over the United States, Europe, Asia and South America, from Paris Fashion Week to Coachella, and everywhere in between.  As an avid nature and animal lover, Harley was drawn to the Offset collection because of its striking images of wildlife, and decided to theme her curated collection around them.
