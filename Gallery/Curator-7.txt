Scott Braut & Keren Sachs|50|Offset Content|Scott Braut and Keren Sachs represent Shutterstock and Offset’s content team as Vice President of Content and Head of Photo Acquisition.  They have a combined 35 years of experience in photography, digital media and publishing.  Scott has worked with The New York Times Upfront, Corbis, and ABC News / Disney.  Keren has held senior creative roles, working with leading photographers at National Geographic, Williams-Sonoma, Martha Stewart and Trunk Archive. 

Putting together this curated collection, they said, “Our goal was to find images that have a beautiful and simple honesty, but are also highly evocative, immersive and engaging.  We are inspired by the Offset artists and we are grateful that they are sharing their work with us.”
