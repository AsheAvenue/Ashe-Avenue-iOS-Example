Liz McDaniel|40|Brand Communications Director|Liz McDaniel is a writer and comedian. She has written personal essays and fashion coverage for The New York Times, WSJ Magazine, Glamour.com, and Style.com, and performs regularly at Gotham Comedy Club. By day, she is the voice of a major fashion brand and was recently named one of the “35 under 35 in Fashion,” by Glamour Magazine. She lives in a tiny apartment at the top of a lot of stairs in Manhattan’s West Village. 

In talking about the images in her curated collection, Liz said, “I tried to choose images that have a sense of humor and that touch on a few of the things I love: city life, funny people, fashion, and dogs.”
